❓ Before You Contact Us

Check if your question is answered below:

🔊 "A pronunciation sounds wrong"

Our sounds are generated by Google Cloud AI (Neural2/Wavenet). Some onomatopoeia may sound different from what you expect because AI interprets spelling through each language's phonetic rules. If you believe there's a genuine error, please email us with the specific sound and country.

🌍 "Can you add my country/language?"

We're always looking to expand! Send us the country name, language, and example sounds. We'll evaluate whether Google Cloud TTS supports high-quality voices for that language.

📱 "The sound doesn't play on my device"

Sound playback depends on your browser, device, and network connection. Try refreshing the page, using a different browser (Chrome recommended), or checking your device's volume settings.

🏫 "Can I use Hello Sounds in my classroom?"

Absolutely! Hello Sounds is free for educational use. Teachers and speech therapists are welcome to use our platform in their lessons. We'd love to hear how you use it — drop us an email!
